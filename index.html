About

You are the keeper of a fledgling dungeon. Gather resources, house creatures, and expand your underground empire. Keep your creatures fed or watch them desert you — or worse.

How to Play

Start by gathering wood manually. Build a Lair to house your first creatures, then stockpile food to attract them. Assign creatures to buildings — each Farm worker produces 2 food per second, but every creature consumes 1. Expand carefully: starvation kills.
